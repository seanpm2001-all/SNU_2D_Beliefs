Major rules

1. No brigading

This is a safe haven for people to freely discuss religious topics. Any sort of brigading/raiding will result in an account suspension. Spamming messages, posting unrelated/hateful memes, or even talking about a raid is a bannable action

2. No hate speech

Hate speech is not allowed in this section of the site. Posting hateful comments towards people in any group in a malicious manner will result in an account suspension, and depending on the offense, can result in being put on a public blacklist, or even an IP ban.
